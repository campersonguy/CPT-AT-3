About Us


Learn about this website's very normal backstory




confessions.com started as a funny silly little class project between its founders, Cameron Guglielmino and Zachariah Conyngham. However, the two decided that this little project deserved more than being presented to the class once. As such, confessions.com became somewhat of a passion project for the two, eventually continued by Cameron alone. This led to the official release of confessions.com in October of 2025, from a questionable PowerPoint presentation to the class encouraging organised crime to a truly impressive website that Mr Clark should definitely give an A for.
At first, confessions.com seemed like nothing more than a chaotic project as the result of leaving two mentally deranged people to present whatever they wanted to the class. However, aside from all the organised crime promotion (RIP Crime Chats), confessions.com showed promise of an actually decent website. As such, Cameron continued the project as a legacy, vowing to turn confessions.com from chaos into greatness. During mid-2025, the project took over him, becoming his only goal as he "grinded" for hours on end to make confessions.com what it is today.
